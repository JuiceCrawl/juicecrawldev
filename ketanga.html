What is a Ketanga Juice Krawl?

An awesome joint event by Juice Crawl and Ketanga Fitness. Juice Crawl shows you the juice bars and Ketanga shows you the fitness studios. On this event you'll get to experience both fitness hopping and juice bar crawling.

Hang with fellow juice lovers, yogis, runners, health conscious individuals etc... Anyone is welcome! Experience a hangover that leaves you feeling energized and healthy.
